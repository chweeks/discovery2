Despite the explosion of screens, the TV set remains the
preferred viewing experience

immersion, comfort – the TV set for many can’t be
beaten, and will remain top of the screen hierarchy
for years to come.

The TV set still plays a vital and unique role in
bringing family and friends together

“The TV set is relaxing and comfortable and not so
distracting as the computer....It just feels cosy...”

“It brings the family together, and creates fond
memories for everybody!”
